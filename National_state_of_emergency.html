Republic of Ivanistan

National state of emergency

Our national parliament has declared a state wide emergency because of the global epidemic of COVID-19.

Government measures for the stopping of COVID-19 are:

Curfew every day from 5pm till 5am and saturdays and sundays are under 24h curfew.

All schools are closing till the end of the emergency.

All mass gatherings are to be delayed.No more than two adults can be together.

This system will last till 16 of june 2020
