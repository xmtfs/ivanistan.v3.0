Republic of Ivanistan

Diplomacy

Diplomacy is a very important tool for all micronations for their self development and recognition.
As such the Republic of Ivanistan is seeking recognition from other micronations.
Intermicronational activity is among the cornerstones of micronationalism, and Ivanistan welcomes contact with any and all states, whether they are sovereign states, political exercises, simulations, or games.
However, because of the Ivanistan's own status as an aspiring state and nation, it follows a strict diplomatic policy as to which states it will and will not recognise.
Ivanistan recognizes all states on tree conditions:
1.recognition is reciprocated,
2.that a applicant does have a constitution for more than 2 months before application for recognition,
3.the constitution must be in compliance with UN human rights

All diplomatic contact must not be written in capital letters and or use swear words and threats.
